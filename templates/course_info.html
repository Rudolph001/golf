Playing Tips

Wind Conditions

The coastal location means wind is a constant factor. Pay attention to wind direction and strength, especially on elevated holes.

Course Management

Strategic course management is key. The dramatic cliff-top setting can be distracting - focus on your target and club selection.

Signature Holes

Hole 16: The most challenging hole - long par 5 with dramatic elevation changes.

Hole 17: Spectacular par 3 over cliff-top terrain with ocean views.

Scoring Strategy

The front nine offers more birdie opportunities, while the back nine tests your course management skills.
